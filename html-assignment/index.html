About me

Hello! I'm Sonia Romero, I'm 32 years old and I'm from Mexico. I was born in Acapulco, a little beach town in the pacific ocean. On my 9th birthday, my family moved to Cancun, which is located in the CAR. This is where I spent most of my adolescence.

To be honest, I don't like beach towns. You are constantly sweating because the temperatures are hot and you cannot go to the beach every day (Because you have to go to work). I much prefer the woods and cold rainy climates. This is why I decided to move to Canada and so far I love it. I also enjoy hiking and camping, and those activities seem more enjoyable here in these weather conditions.

“A change in the weather is sufficient to recreate the world and ourselves”. -Marcel Proust
